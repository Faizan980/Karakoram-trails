The beauty of Karakoram is because of it mesmerizing mountains.

The Karakoram is a mountain range in Kashmir region spanning the borders of Pakistan, China, and India, with the northwestern extremity of the range extending to Afghanistan and Tajikistan. Most of the Karakoram mountain range falls under the jurisdiction of Gilgit-Baltistan, which is administered by Pakistan.

The snow leopard, also known as the "ghost of the mountains," is a highly elusive and magnificent big cat species found in the Karakoram Range of Pakistan. It is a symbol of the region's unique biodiversity and is considered one of the most beautiful and charismatic animals on the planet.
